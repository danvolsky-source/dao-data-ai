## 1. Introduction

DAO Data AI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Platform, we automatically collect:

#### Technical Information:
- **IP Address:** For security, rate limiting, and geographic analytics
- **User Agent:** Browser type, version, and operating system
- **Device Information:** Device type, screen resolution
- **Access Times:** Date and time of Platform access
- **Referral URLs:** Source of traffic to the Platform

#### Usage Information:
- **API Endpoints Accessed:** Which features you use
- **Query Parameters:** Search terms and filters used
- **Response Times:** Performance monitoring
- **Error Logs:** For debugging and improvement

### 2.2 Blockchain Data

#### Public On-Chain Data:
- **Wallet Addresses:** If you connect a wallet (public by nature)
- **Transaction Hashes:** Public blockchain transactions
- **Voting History:** Public governance participation
- **Token Holdings:** Public blockchain data

**Important:** Blockchain data is PUBLIC and PERMANENT. We do not control blockchain data.

### 2.3 Voluntarily Provided Information

You may choose to provide:
- **Email Address:** For notifications (if you opt-in)
- **Discord Username:** For sentiment analysis correlation
- **Forum Username:** For community analysis

### 2.4 Information We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Private keys or seed phrases
- Passwords
- Financial account information
- Social Security numbers
- Credit card information
- Personal identification documents

### 4.2 Security Measures
